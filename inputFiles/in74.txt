Leg cramps - metrohex gel,bonheal capsule(To be taken under guidance of licensed practitioner)
The cramps last longer than 10 minutes and fail to improve, despite exercise.	Cramps develop after you come into contact with substances that could be toxic (poisonous) or infectious, for example, if you have a cut that is contaminated with soil, which can sometimes cause a bacterial infection, such as tetanus, or after being exposed to elements such as mercury or lead.
