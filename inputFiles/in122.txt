Face – the face may have dropped on one side, the person may not be able to smile, or their mouth or eye may have drooped.	Arms – the person with suspected stroke may not be able to lift both arms and keep them there, because of arm weakness or numbness in one arm.	Speech – their speech may be slurred or garbled, or the person may not be able to talk at all, despite appearing to be awake.
