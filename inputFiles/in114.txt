Skin cancer (melanoma) - Doctor must be consulted and after correct diagnosis, proper medication is to be taken 
getting bigger	changing shape	changing colour	bleeding or becoming crusty	itchy or painful	Asymmetrical – melanomas have 2 very different halves and are an irregular shape.	Border – melanomas have a notched or ragged border.	Colours – melanomas will be a mix of 2 or more colours.	Diameter – melanomas are larger than 6mm (1/4 inch) in diameter.	Enlargement or elevation – a mole that changes size over time is more likely to be a melanoma.	Cancer Research UK: Melanoma symptoms.
